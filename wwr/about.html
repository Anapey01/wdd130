About Us - Water Rafting Company

At Whitewater Adventures, we create unforgettable rafting experiences that connect you with nature's raw power.

Our History

Established in 2000, Whitewater Adventures began as a small, family-owned business fueled by a passion for rafting and a deep appreciation for the outdoors. Founders John and Mary Thompson started with just two rafts and a vision to share the excitement and beauty of white water rafting with others.

Since then, we have grown into a leading rafting company in the region, while staying true to our core values: safety, adventure, and environmental stewardship. Our commitment is to deliver memorable experiences and uphold the highest standards of safety and respect for nature.
